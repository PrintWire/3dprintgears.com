About 3D Print Gears

Covering additive manufacturing for gears and drivetrain additive manufacturing — from concept to production hardware.

OUR STORY

Drivetrains at the Frontier of Fabrication

3D Print Gears covers additive manufacturing for power transmission — from planetary and cycloidal reducers to helical, worm, and bevel gearing and the drivetrains they build.

Our editorial team includes mechanical and drivetrain engineers with real project experience across reducers, robotics gearing, and motion systems.

PURPOSE

Our Mission

To cover gear AM from the printed tooth to the full drivetrain with equal depth — the gear, the reducer, and the material behind them.

Our Vision

To be the authoritative, vendor-independent source for additive manufacturing in gearing — connecting builders to the suppliers reshaping how drivetrains get made.

WHAT DRIVES US

What Sets Us Apart

⚙ Whole-Drivetrain

From a single tooth to a full gearbox — we cover gear AM across the drivetrain.

◆ Process-Literate
